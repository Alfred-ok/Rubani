About Rubani Sacco

Rubani Sacco was founded in October 2013 with the aim of empowering aviation professionals through affordable and dependable financial services. We began with a Ksh 100,000 boost from KALPA and officially started member contributions in February 2014.

Today, we proudly serve over 300 members including Pilots, Engineers, Cabin Crew, and aviation staff from JKIA, Wilson Airport, and international bases such as Doha.

Our members’ savings have grown beyond Ksh 300 Million and our loan portfolio exceeds Ksh 190 Million.

Our Vision

To be a world-class Sacco offering the best financial services and products to our members.

Our Mission

To provide efficient, reliable and secure Sacco facilities through modern technology and excellent customer care, with utmost focus on integrity, transparency, timeliness, professionalism and accountability.
